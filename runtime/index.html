How to play

Rules and controls

Player 1
W/S or Arrow keys move • mouse or touch-drag also steers • F or Space fires (Slash and Right Ctrl work too in single player)
Player 2
Arrow Up and Arrow Down move • Slash, Right Ctrl, or Numpad 0 fires
Touch
Drag the floating joystick up or down to move • tap anywhere else on the play area to launch a wave (the strongest one you can afford)
Control scheme
Choose Wave Control in the menu. Hold to Charge: hold fire to build a stronger wave, then release to fire. Auto-Fire: every press instantly launches the strongest wave you can afford.
Pick your wave (Hold to Charge)
Tap fire for BLUE • a brief hold and release fires PINK • hold longer for GOLD. The ring at your paddle shows what will fire.
Gamepad
Left stick or D-pad moves • A (or right trigger) fires and follows your chosen Wave Control scheme • Start pauses
Pause
P or Escape pauses the match
Mute
M toggles sound
Wave costs
Blue uses 10 percent charge • Pink uses 50 percent • Gold uses 90 percent. You can only fire a tier you can afford, so the notches on your charge bar mark pink and gold readiness.
Wave roles
Blue is flexible control, pink is defensive coverage, and gold is the high impact finisher
XP raises wave level over time; leveling up also refunds a burst of charge. Conceding a goal grants some XP and charge back, so you are never locked out of the fight. Powerups apply instantly, and some temporary buffs also change charge capacity or recharge speed.
